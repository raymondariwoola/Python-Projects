# Various conversions

### convert Year to any

1. Years to Months
2. Years to Days
3. Years to hours
4. Years to minutes
5. Years to seconds

## Usage

```python
convertDate = convert_YDHM(1)

# Years to Months
convertDate.convert_years_to_months()

# Years to Days
convertDate.convert_years_to_days()

# Years to hours
convertDate.convert_years_to_hours()

# Years to minutes
convertDate.convert_years_to_minutes()

# Years to seconds
convertDate.convert_years_to_seconds()
```
